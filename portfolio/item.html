Freezer Friend

What's in your fridge?

In this project, My partners Gerald Bartlett, Barry Poole and I created a protoype of an application that allows the user to monitor the goods inside the fridge or freezer. With a quick check of the app, an up- to-date tally of the fridge or freezer's items can be retrieved in a couple clicks.

As an example, here in the fridge we can see the items we have at home. While out at the grocery store, if we are unsure if we have sour cream, we can cimply grab our phomne, open our app and see for ouselves.

The freezer friend is a way for the consumers to save themselves from making extra trips to the grocery store or buying items that they may already have.
